## Research Focus
My research explores how AI can operate safely in autonomous contexts. I focus on:
- **Bounded autonomy** – defining formal action taxonomies that allow models to take reversible actions autonomously while ensuring irreversible actions remain under human oversight.  
- **Constraint validation** – ensuring models adhere to safety constraints and governance policies during deployment and learning.  
- **Multi‑agent coordination** – developing protocols that allow distributed agents to form collective hypotheses while preserving dissent and maintaining safety invariants.
